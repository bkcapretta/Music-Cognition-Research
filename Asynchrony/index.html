ACTIVITY A

INSTRUCTIONS: You will participate in a 40 second tapping activity with the experimenter. You will be assigned the BLUE button. You must click the button every time it appears on your half of the screen. You earn one point for every time you accurately press the button while it is on the screen, and the experimenter earns one point every time they accurately press their button. You two will be working together to receive a team score at the end of the activity.
