Perceived Time Experiment

INSTRUCTIONS: You will make judgements of the duration of the interval between the act of pressing the green "PRESS" button and the following tone in milliseconds. The tone will occur somewhere between 1 and 1000ms after you press the button (no longer than one second). 1000ms is 1 second and 500ms is 0.5 seconds.

After the tone, speak your estimate out loud and then enter it below. You will go through 30 self-paced trials. For each, please try to give your most accurate guess.
